Privacy Policy

SnapTrack · Last updated: June 2026

SnapTrack ("we", "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Information we collect

• Account info: if you sign in with Google, your name, email and profile photo. Guests are identified only by an anonymous, hashed device ID — never your raw device identifiers.

• Health & profile data you enter: age, gender, height, weight and calorie goals, used to calculate your targets.

• Meal data: photos you capture/upload for analysis, and the resulting food items, calories and macros you log.

• Usage data: app diagnostics and crash logs to keep the app working.

How we use your data

To analyze meal photos, calculate nutrition, sync your logs across devices, send reminders you enable, and improve reliability.

AI photo processing

Meal photos are sent to AI providers (Google Vertex AI / Gemini, and fallback vision providers) solely to identify food and estimate nutrition. Images are processed in-memory and are not used to identify you.

Storage & third parties

Your account data and logs are stored using Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage). We use Google AdMob to show ads to free users, and Google Play Billing for subscriptions. These providers process data under their own privacy policies.

Ads

Free accounts see ads served by Google AdMob, which may use advertising identifiers. Upgrading to a paid plan removes ads.

Data retention & deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete Account, which permanently removes your logs, profile and account data from our systems.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country).

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at theteamvesper@gmail.com.
